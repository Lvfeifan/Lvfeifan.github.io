About Me

I am Feifan Lv, and currently a master student in the School of Computer Science & Engineering of Beihang University, Beijing, China. I am working in the PHI-AI Lab which is affiliated with the State Key Laboratory of Virtual Reality Technology and Systems, under the supervision of prof. Feng Lu.

My research interests lie primarily in the area of Computer Vision and Image processing. To be specific, I'm particularly interested in Image/Video Enhancement and Restoration using Deep Learning Methods.
